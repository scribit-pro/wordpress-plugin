Online video from your WordPress site accessible to all.

== Description ==
We're making it even easier to publish accessible videos on your WordPress site! With the Scribit.Pro WordPress plugin
you'll be able to add Audio Descriptions and Text Alternatives to your YouTube and Vimeo videos without any coding.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= What is Scribit.Pro? =
Online video accessible to all. That’s us, Scribit.Pro. Hi there. Our software enables you to turn your video into an accessible video. And, what’s more, that video can also be published in an accessible manner. This way everyone will be able to understand your video.
With our user-friendly online editor you can easily add subtitling, audio description and text alternatives to your video. Providing these files means your video complies with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG). You can use the editor yourself, or you can let our pros do the job for you.

= Why do online videos need to be accessible? =
Lots of people have problems understanding videos that are inaccessible. This concerns the deaf and hard of hearing, people who are blind or visually impaired, but also people with reading difficulties or cognitive disabilities. They need subtitles, audio description, text alternatives or sign language translations in order to understand audiovisual content. Your accessible video therefore not only provides you with a larger target group and a greater reach, it also improves your SEO. In addition, your message will have more impact.

== Installation ==
1. Activate the plugin in WordPress.
2. Configure your Scribit ID in the settings page
3. Make your video accessible using scribit.pro
4. Done! when pasting a YouTube or Vimeo link of your video, the plugin wil automatically display the published Audio Descriptions and Text Alternatives.
